Unit EU

Mensuration is a system to quantify measurements in real life. Measures like perimeter, area, surface area and volume help us solve optimisation problems involving 2D or 3D spaces.

Unit EQs

● What is perimeter?

● What is area?

● What is volume?

● What is surface area?

Unit Objectives

● Calculate perimeter and area of composite plane figures, including parallelogram and trapezium

● Calculate volume and surface area of composite solids, including cube, cuboid, prism, cylinder, pyramid, cone and sphere

● Convert between cm2 and m2, and between cm3 and m3
