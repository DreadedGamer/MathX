UNIT 2.2: Substitution Method

A familiar example that you will come across will be as follow.

Marcus bought 7 pens and 9 pencils for $39.

Tim bought 3 pens and 2 pencils for $13.

What is the price of 1 pen and 1 pencil respectively?
